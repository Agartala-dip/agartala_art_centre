Welcome to Agartala Art Centre

Agartala Art Centre is more than just a drawing school — it’s a vibrant hub of creativity, imagination, and artistic excellence. Founded with the vision of nurturing young talent and promoting fine arts among all age groups, our centre has become a trusted name in Agartala's art education landscape.

Our school offers a wide range of art programs, from basic drawing and painting techniques to advanced workshops in various mediums like watercolour, acrylic, oil, charcoal, and more. Each class is guided by experienced mentors and tailored to meet the individual needs of every student.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To foster artistic expression and creative growth through structured art education and hands-on experiences.

Vision: To build a community of artists who inspire, express, and elevate the world through their artwork.

Why Choose Agartala Art Centre?

  • ✅ Structured drawing syllabus for all levels
  • ✅ Individual attention and portfolio development
  • ✅ Year-round exhibitions, contests & workshops
  • ✅ Affiliated with Tripura Art Society
