Agartala Art Centre is a visionary initiative founded to cultivate creativity, imagination, and fine arts skills in children, youths, and adults. Located in the heart of Bardowali, Madhya Para, Agartala, our centre has been playing a pivotal role in nurturing artistic talents and promoting visual art education in Tripura.

Affiliated with the esteemed Tripura Art Society, our art centre offers structured courses in drawing, painting, craft, and design. From beginners to advanced learners, our tailored programs and hands-on guidance help individuals build strong artistic foundations and explore their unique styles.

Our founder, faculty, and visiting artists are passionate educators committed to fostering not just technical skills, but also confidence, expression, and emotional well-being through art. We also integrate art therapy and inclusive teaching strategies, especially for children with diverse learning needs.

Throughout the year, Agartala Art Centre organizes exhibitions, contests, art camps, and workshops that inspire our students and showcase their creativity to the wider community.
